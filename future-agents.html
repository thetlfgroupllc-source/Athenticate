The Lines We Don't Cross

What We
Will Never Do.

Athlete trust is the platform. The features below will never exist on ATHenticate, regardless of agent demand or revenue impact. Our credibility with athletes depends on these lines staying drawn.

On pricing: Pricing for all agent tiers will be announced at launch. Founding agents who claim their profile early will be notified directly when subscriptions open.

Paid Search Placement

Agents are listed by athlete-facing criteria like rating and review recency. Payment never affects search ranking, visibility, or recommendation.

Replies to Reviews

Agents cannot publicly respond to reviews. The accountability loop depends on athletes being heard without a counter-narrative shaping their words.

Reviewer Identifiers

Agents see review content. They never see names, metadata, or any signal that could compromise athlete anonymity, even at the highest tier.

Hiding or Suppressing Reviews

Agents cannot hide, delay, or remove ratings. The dispute process exists for reviews that violate Terms of Service. Negative reviews stay.

Promotional Profile Copy

Profile content is factual: credentials, certifications, specialties, years experience. No marketing claims. No persuasive language. No "best agent" badges.

Bypassing Athlete Consent

Agents cannot contact athletes outside athlete-initiated channels. Even Elite tier discovery requires the athlete to opt in. The athlete controls every conversation.
