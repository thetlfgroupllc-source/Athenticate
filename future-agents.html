Full Feature Vision

Everything Being
Built for Agents

When agent features launch, here's the full picture of what a verified agent subscription will include.

Verified Agent Profile

Claim your profile and verify credentials — agency, certifications, sports, years active. Include services offered, your background, and client success stories. Your reviews are written by players. That's the credibility no self-reported profile can match.

Searchable by Athletes

Professional athletes and college athletes preparing to sign will search the platform by sport, location, specialty, and experience. A strong verified profile means visibility exactly when athletes are making decisions.

College Athlete Database

Access profiles of college athletes preparing to go pro — sport, position, school, eligibility year, stats, highlights, and representation preferences. Filter by sport, draft year, and profile criteria.

Secure Messaging & Video Calls

Communicate directly with athletes through secure in-app messaging and scheduled video calls — without exchanging personal contact information. Both sides set availability and request conversations on their schedule.

Profile Analytics

See how athletes are engaging with your profile — views, saves, and interaction data. Understand how your reputation is landing and optimize how you present yourself on the platform.

Performance Rankings

Your position in the rankings is determined entirely by verified player reviews — communication, contracts, loyalty, availability, and career guidance. Results over relationships.

Social Media Integration

Connect your social media profiles to your agent page so athletes can see your public presence, engagement, and how you represent your brand and clients in the open market.

Content & Education Tools

Publish blogs, insights, and educational content on the platform about athlete representation. Build credibility and connect with athletes through shared knowledge — not just a profile page.

Webinars & Networking Events

Participate in virtual events where agents and athletes connect directly. Host or join webinars that position you as an expert and build genuine relationships with prospects at scale.

Peer Referral System

Athletes who have a great experience with you can recommend you directly to other players on the platform. Organic endorsements that carry more weight than any marketing you could do yourself.

Integrity & Reporting Tools

A transparent review and reporting mechanism ensures the platform maintains integrity. Reviews can be flagged for review, and agents have a clear process for addressing disputes through ATHenticate's moderation team.

Multi-Sport Support

Represent athletes across multiple leagues? Your profile reflects every sport you work in — with league-specific breakdowns, review filters, and separate ranking visibility per sport.

Our Position
"ATHenticate isn't built against agents. It's built to reward the ones who deserve it."

Good agents have nothing to fear from transparency — they have everything to gain. A verified profile with strong reviews from real clients is more powerful than any pitch. The agents who consistently show up for their players will be the ones this platform elevates. Agent features are paid because access to athletes — and the credibility this platform provides — is worth investing in.
