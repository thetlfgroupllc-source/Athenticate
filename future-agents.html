What Agent Features
Will Include

Verified Profile

Claim your profile and verify credentials — agency, certifications, sports represented, years of experience. Built from player reviews, not self-reported claims.

Performance Rankings

Your ranking is determined entirely by verified player reviews — communication, contracts, loyalty, availability. Results over relationships.

Athlete Database Access

Agents with strong records can access future professional profiles — sport, position, school, eligibility, highlights, and agent preferences.

Direct Messaging & Video

Communicate with athletes through secure in-app messaging and video calls. No personal contact information exchanged. Both sides set availability and schedule on their terms.

Strength Tags

Positive patterns across multiple reviews surface automatically — responsive, strong negotiator, real relationships, long-term thinker, transparent on fees.

Profile Analytics

See how athletes interact with your profile — views, engagement, and performance metrics that help you understand your visibility and optimize your presence.

Our Position

Good agents have nothing to fear from transparency. A verified profile with strong, honest reviews from real professional clients is more powerful than any pitch. The agents who consistently show up for their players will be the ones this platform elevates.
