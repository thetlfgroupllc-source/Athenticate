What's Being Built for You

Features Coming
for Future Professionals

On pricing: Submitting reviews is always free for professional athletes. Access to the full platform — agent search, profiles, matching, messaging, and educational resources — requires a subscription.

Verified Profile

Create a profile with your sport, position, school, eligibility, stats, highlights, and agent preferences. Visible to verified agents actively seeking new clients.

Full Agent Search

Search any agent by name, sport, location, specialty, or experience level. Read verified reviews from professional athletes who've worked with them.

Compatibility Matching

An algorithm matches you with agents based on your sport, career stage, goals, and preferences — surfacing the most relevant connections, not just the most popular.

Direct Messaging & Video

Communicate with agents through secure in-app messaging and video calls. Set availability windows and schedule calls on your terms — no personal contact shared.

Highlight & Social Links

Link your Hudl, YouTube, or social media directly to your profile so agents can see your game and public presence before making contact.

Combine & Draft Prep Resources

A library of articles, webinars, and workshops covering combine prep, NFL and NBA draft process, contract understanding, and managing your personal brand.

Red Flag Alerts

Warning patterns that appear across multiple player reviews surface automatically — so you see the red flags before you sign anything.

Peer Referral System

Athletes recommend agents to each other based on real experiences. The most credible recommendation you can get — from a player who's been where you are.
