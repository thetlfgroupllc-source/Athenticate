What's Being Built for You

Features Coming
for Future Professionals

Athlete Transparency
Every agent watching you is visible to you, by name, with their reviews, before any contact happens. You see the interest. You decide what to do with it.

On pricing: Submitting reviews is always free for professional athletes. Access to the full platform (agent search, profiles, matching, messaging, and educational resources) requires a subscription.

Verified Profile + Stealth Mode

Build a profile with your sport, position, school, stats, highlights, and stated preferences. Whatever you put up is visible to subscribed agents. Or switch to Stealth Mode to stay hidden until you're ready to be seen.

Full Agent Search

Search any agent by name, sport, location, specialty, or experience level. Read verified reviews from professional athletes who work or have worked with them.

Smart Matching With Reasoning

The platform surfaces relevant agents using a balanced mix of agent track record, your stated preferences, and review scores from athletes at similar career stages. You see why each agent matches. Never a fit score telling you who to pick. Your preferences stay private until you choose to engage.

See Who's Watching You

Every agent who watchlists you is visible to you, by name, with their full review profile. Before they ever reach you, you know who's interested and what other athletes have said about them. Agents only see a number on their end. The visibility runs in your favor.

Athlete-Initiated Communication

Agents cannot message you first. When you decide to open communication, secure in-app messaging and video scheduling unlock. No phone numbers, no personal email, no contact outside the platform. Mute, close, or end any conversation instantly.

Highlight & Social Links

Link your Hudl, YouTube, or social media directly to your profile so agents can see your game and public presence before making contact.

Combine & Draft Prep Resources

A library of articles, webinars, and workshops covering combine prep, NFL and NBA draft process, contract understanding, and managing your personal brand.

Red Flag Alerts

Warning patterns that appear across multiple player reviews surface automatically, so you see the red flags before you sign anything.

Peer Referral System

Athletes recommend agents to each other based on real experiences. The most credible recommendation you can get, from a player who is or has been where you are.
