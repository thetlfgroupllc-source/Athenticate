Frequently Asked.
Anonymity & Privacy

Is my review really anonymous?

Yes. Your review is published without any identifying information. No name, no handle, no league — nothing that connects it back to you publicly. The verification contact you provide is stored separately and used only to confirm you are a professional athlete.

Can my agent find out I submitted a review?

No. Agents do not have access to reviewer identities under any circumstance. Your verification contact is never accessible to agents or any third party.

What do you do with my Instagram or email?

Used solely to verify you are a professional athlete. We may reach out once to confirm your submission. We do not add you to any mailing list, share your contact with third parties, or use it for marketing.

Submitting a Review

What happens after I submit?

Your review goes through a manual verification process. Once confirmed, it contributes to your league's database. When enough reviews exist in your sport, full access unlocks for everyone in that league.

Is this only for negative experiences?

Not at all. ATHenticate is built to reward the right agents and hold the rest accountable. If your agent was excellent, that record matters just as much. Great agents deserve to be found.

Can I review more than one agent?

Yes. If you have worked with multiple agents, each experience is worth recording. Submit a separate review for each agent.

Can I edit or remove my review after submitting?

Yes. Contact us at [email protected] and we will process your request within 30 days. Include the agent name and the verification contact you used when submitting.

The Platform

Is it free to submit a review?

Always. Submitting a review is free for every professional athlete in every league. Full platform access requires a subscription when those features launch.
